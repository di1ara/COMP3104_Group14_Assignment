    George Brown College was founded in 1967 and is one of the largest colleges in Ontario.
    It is named after George Brown, a prominent 19th-century Canadian journalist, politician, and one of the Fathers of Confederation.George Brown College offers a wide range of academic programs, including diplomas, certificates, postgraduate certificates, degree programs, and continuing education courses.The college is located in the heart of Toronto, one of Canada's largest and most vibrant cities. This offers students access to a wide range of cultural, social, and employment opportunities.
