Title: COMP3104 - DevOps Course Information

The COMP 3104 DevOps course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of DevOps principles, practices, and tools. This course explores the intersection of development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops), emphasizing collaboration, automation, and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.

Course Objectives:-------------------
- Understand the principles and benefits of DevOps.
- Gain proficiency in Git and version control.
- Build and automate CI/CD pipelines.
- Learn to manage infrastructure as code.
- Implement containerization and orchestration.
- Explore monitoring, logging, and security in a DevOps environment.
